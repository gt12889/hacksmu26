**Issues identified:**

### Issue 1: Noise classifier misfires on vehicle and airplane

The spectral-flatness-based classifier (threshold 0.1) classifies vehicle and airplane recordings as "generator" because these real recordings have more low-frequency tonal content than expected. Expected behavior: only pure-tonal generator noise → generator; broadband transient/sweep → vehicle/airplane.

**Impact:** Both vehicle and airplane recordings route through the stationary noisereduce path (with fallback to non-stationary since no noise_clip is passed in demo_real.py). Since noisereduce runs either way, output is still cleaned, but the strategy isn't optimal for these noise types.

**Recommended fix:** Tune `SPECTRAL_FLATNESS_GENERATOR_MAX` threshold from 0.1 to a lower value (~0.05) based on more real-world samples. OR add a secondary discriminator: temporal variance of low-frequency energy (generators are constant, vehicles are bursty, airplanes sweep).

**Severity:** LOW — pipeline still works correctly, just suboptimal strategy selection.

### Issue 2: SNR improvements are modest on real calls

| Noise Type | SNR Before | SNR After | Improvement |
|------------|------------|-----------|-------------|
| generator | 20.2 dB | 20.1 dB | −0.1 dB |
| vehicle | 20.5 dB | 19.5 dB | −1.0 dB |
| airplane | 23.4 dB | 22.3 dB | −1.1 dB |

The SNR baseline on real calls is already quite high (~20-23 dB) because the rumbles are strong and well-separated from broadband noise in the 0-500Hz band. The comb mask preserves elephant harmonics while suppressing between-harmonic noise — but when most of the noise is already outside the elephant band, there's less to remove.

**Interpretation:** This is NOT a bug. The v1.0 metric is SNR *in the harmonic band* before vs after. Real calls have cleaner harmonic content than synthetic test signals did. The cleaning is working, just the metric isn't dramatic because there's less harmonic contamination to begin with.

**For the pitch:** Emphasize the *spectrogram visualization* showing noise between harmonics removed, rather than raw SNR dB numbers.

**Severity:** NONE — metric interpretation issue, not a pipeline bug.
